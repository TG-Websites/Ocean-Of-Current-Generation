Our Story

Since our journey began in 2010, we have been committed to one mission — empowering people with reliable energy. Our company was founded with the vision of bringing together the best of solar technology and advanced genset solutions to meet the growing energy needs of homes, businesses, and industries.

Over the years, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of clean and renewable solar power, helping families reduce electricity costs and contribute to a greener planet. At the same time, our modern diesel and gas generators ensure uninterrupted power supply during outages, giving industries and critical facilities the peace of mind they deserve.

With a focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we have delivered solutions that are both sustainable and dependable. Whether it’s harnessing the power of the sun or ensuring backup with gensets, we provide end-to-end services — from consultation and installation to long-term maintenance.
