About Us

Ocean of Current Generation (OPC) Private Limited has been dedicated to delivering reliable solar, generator, and electrical solutions across homes, businesses, and industries. Headquartered in Haryana, we specialize in energy-efficient technologies that help customers reduce costs and move towards a sustainable future.

We also provide battery trading, foundation works, and advanced electrical solutions. With a strong team of professionals, we focus on high-quality products, expert installation, and ongoing maintenance support. Our mission is to power communities with clean, dependable, and innovative energy solutions for a better tomorrow.
