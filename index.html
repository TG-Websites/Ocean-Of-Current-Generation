Who We Are

Ocean of Current Generation (OPC) Private Limited has been dedicated to delivering reliable solar, generator, and electrical solutions across homes, businesses, and industries. We are headquartered in Haryana and specialize in energy-efficient technologies that help customers save costs and move towards a sustainable future.

We also provide battery trading, foundation works, and advanced electrical solutions. With a strong team of professionals, we focus on high-quality products, expert installation, and ongoing maintenance support. Our mission is to power communities with clean, dependable, and innovative energy solutions for a better tomorrow.

About Us

Ocean of Current Generation (OPC) Private Limited began with a simple goal: to bring reliable and sustainable energy solutions to every home and business.

Over the years, we have grown from a small startup into a trusted name in the industry, driven by a team passionate about innovation, technology, and environmental responsibility.

Our vision is to create a world where energy is clean, accessible, and efficient for everyone. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to quality, integrity, and sustainability.

We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our customers and communities, fostering trust and shared progress on the journey to a greener future.
